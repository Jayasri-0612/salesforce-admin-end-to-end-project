# Business Scenario

## Project Title
**Smart Service & Sales Management System (SSSMS)**

## Business Problem
A company provides products along with after-sales services. The company needs a Salesforce solution to manage both sales and service operations efficiently.

The business wants Salesforce to:

- Manage customers and their contacts
- Track sales orders
- Handle customer service requests
- Assign technicians to service requests
- Track technician work and service costs
- Record payments after service completion
- Enforce data security
- Maintain data quality
- Automate processes using Salesforce Flows
- Provide insights through reports and dashboards

## Salesforce Solution Overview
To address these needs, Salesforce is implemented using standard and custom objects along with automation, security, and reporting features.

The solution enables:
- End-to-end service request tracking
- Automated calculations and validations
- Secure access for different user roles
- Reduced manual effort through automation
- Accurate reporting for business decision-making

## Interview Explanation (Short Version)
“This project is a real-world Salesforce Admin implementation where I designed a complete service and sales management system. It covers data modeling, security, automation, validation, and reporting to support after-sales service operations.”

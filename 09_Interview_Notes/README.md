## Key Talking Points

1. **Business Problem:** End-to-end service and sales management
2. **Data Model:** Standard + custom objects with master-detail and lookup relationships
3. **Fields & Formulas:** Automate calculations like Total Cost, Service Duration, and High Priority
4. **Validation Rules:** Ensure data quality and business logic
5. **Security Model:** OWD, Profiles, Permission Sets for role-based access
6. **Automation:** Record-triggered, Screen, and Scheduled Flows
7. **Reports & Dashboards:** Monitor performance and revenue
8. **Benefits:** Automation, accuracy, scalability, and interview-ready

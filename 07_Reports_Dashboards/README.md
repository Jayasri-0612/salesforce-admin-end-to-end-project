# Reports and Dashboards

Reports and dashboards are used to provide business insights and track
service performance in the Smart Service & Sales Management System (SSSMS).

---

## Reports

### 1. Open Service Requests by Priority
- Shows all open service requests grouped by priority
- Helps managers identify urgent issues

### 2. Technician Hours Worked
- Displays total hours worked by each technician
- Used for performance and workload analysis

### 3. Monthly Service Revenue
- Shows total revenue generated from completed services
- Used for financial tracking

---

## Dashboard

### Key Components
- Total Open Service Requests
- Average Resolution Time
- Revenue from Services

**Business Benefit:**  
Provides management with a real-time overview of service operations and performance.
